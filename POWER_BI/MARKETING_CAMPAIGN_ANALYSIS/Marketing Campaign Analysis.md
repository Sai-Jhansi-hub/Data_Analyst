### **Marketing Campaign Analysis Using Power BI**



#### **Problem Statement:**

You are working as a Marketing Analyst, and your task is to study how well different marketing campaigns are performing across various regions and platforms (like digital ads, social media, and traditional marketing).



In this mini project, you need to build a Power BI dashboard that helps visualize the campaign data, compare performance, and highlight key insights such as ROI, revenue, and spending. (Create a Power BI dashboard to understand how different marketing campaigns performed using given data.)



This project will test your skills in data cleaning, modeling, DAX, and dashboard creation.

##### Task 1: Data Preparation :



Import all 3 CSV files into Power BI.

Clean the dataset (rename columns, handle nulls/duplicates, correct data types).

Rename columns for clarity (e.g., "Campaign\_Type", "Total\_Spend", etc.).



#### Task 2: Data Modeling :

Create relationships:

Marketing\_Campaign\_Performance to Marketing\_Campaign\_Details via Campaign Name (One-to-Many).

Marketing\_Campaign\_Performance to Region\_Performance via Region (One-to-Many).





#### Task 3: DAX Measures :



Create the following DAX measures in the Marketing\_Campaign\_Performance table:



Total Impressions

Total Clicks

Total Conversions

Total Revenue

Total Spend

Total ROI

Average ROI

Best Campaign 





#### Task 4: Visualizations :



Design a professional-looking dashboard that includes:



Stacked Column Chart – Total Spend vs Total Revenue by Region

Combo Chart – Total Spend and Average ROI by Campaign

Bar Chart – Total ROI by Campaign

Pie Chart – Number of Campaigns by Campaign Type

Gauge Chart – Total ROI vs Average ROI

KPI Cards for:

Total Revenue

Total Spend

Average ROI

Best Campaign

Add interactivity through slicers for Region and Campaign Type.





Dashboard Design Guidelines :

Use a dark canvas background and light-colored text for contrast.

Maintain consistent font style and formatting.

Use borders, shadows, and spacing to make visuals distinct.

Use the Format Painter tool for consistent styling.
